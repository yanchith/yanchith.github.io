Ján Tóth

I am a systems programmer and an aspiring game maker. Since 2021, I've been leading a team of smart programmers at a startup by day, and developing a video game by night.

In software engineering I believe in first principles thinking, and that programmers should not add artificial complexity to software. I enjoy crafting dedicated tools for each project, and when picking off-the-shelf tech, I choose something simple, so that it can be customized to the project's needs.

As for video games, I'd like to build stuff I would enjoy playing myself. To that end, I like games that mine a lot of experience out of a compact design. I love games that encourage experimentation, where you can look back on your journey as a player and honestly say that this game changed the way your brain is wired. Here's a few games I think do this really well: The Witness, The Talos Principle, Tunic, Enigmash, Animal Well, Outer Wilds.
