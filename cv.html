Ján Tóth

I am a systems programmer and game developer. Between 2021 and 2025, I was leading a small team of programmers at a startup by day, and developing a video game by night. At the moment, I am working on multiple smaller game prototypes.

I strive to maintain a sense of ownership in projects, as that translates into long-term productivity and good team morale. Ownership comes from familiarity and simplicity. To that end, I responsibly evaluate the use of off-the-shelf tech and generative AI, so that the team does not unfavorably trade ownership for a short-term boost.

When programming myself, I solve problems from first principles and try not to add artificial complexity. I lean towards programming in C-like subsets of languages I work with: C++, Rust, JAI, C# and JavaScript. I do not shy away from diving into large complex codebases, but I try to make my own work simple to understand for others.
